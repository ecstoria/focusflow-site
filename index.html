STAY IN
THE ZONE

When you start a session and minimize, FocusFlow disappears from your taskbar and becomes a small floating circle that lives above whatever you're working on.

No clutter. No alt-tabbing to check the clock. Just a subtle, beautiful reminder that you're in focus mode. Click it to return. Right-click to pause or end.

Drag it to any corner of any monitor. FocusFlow remembers where you left it.
